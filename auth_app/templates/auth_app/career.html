Field Investigators

Field investigators are critical assets in our cybersecurity framework. These professionals bridge the gap between digital and physical domains by collecting, analyzing, and documenting vital evidence. Whether it's a compromised server or a breached facility, our investigators dive deep to uncover the truth. This role requires excellent observational skills, sharp critical thinking, and the ability to navigate sensitive environments with discretion. Investigators often collaborate with law enforcement and internal teams, providing actionable intelligence for ongoing operations. If you're passionate about real-world cyber incidents and enjoy investigative work that blends technology with field activity, this role is an ideal opportunity.

Digital Forensics

Our digital forensics team plays a vital role in responding to cybersecurity incidents with precision and technical expertise. These professionals utilize advanced forensic tools and methodologies to analyze compromised systems, recover lost data, and trace threat actors. Responsibilities include conducting forensic imaging, log analysis, malware reverse-engineering, and providing expert testimony when necessary. Accuracy, confidentiality, and deep technical knowledge are key in this role. We’re seeking team members who are methodical, curious, and committed to uncovering digital evidence. If you are excited about diving into the digital trail and playing a key role in breach response, we want to hear from you.

Risk Analysts

Risk analysts at our company are proactive defenders of information security. They assess systems for vulnerabilities, forecast emerging threats, and develop strategic recommendations to mitigate risks. This role involves performing risk assessments, creating threat models, and working closely with various departments to ensure compliance and best practices. An eye for detail, strong analytical skills, and a solid understanding of cybersecurity principles are essential. You’ll help shape our defense strategies and guide decision-makers through risk management processes. If you enjoy problem-solving, predictive analysis, and making data-driven security decisions, this role will put your talents to the test in a meaningful way.
