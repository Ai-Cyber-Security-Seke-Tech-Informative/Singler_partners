Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Singler Partners. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data across our digital platforms. We are committed to maintaining your privacy and confidentiality in line with our values of integrity and accountability.

Last updated: June 2025

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Any personal details voluntarily provided in forms

Non-Personal Information:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Device type and operating system

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to inquiries or career applications
  • To enhance security and protect against misuse
  • To perform analytics for service improvement
  • To notify users of updates or policy changes

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to personalize content, remember your preferences, and analyze traffic. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings.

5. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Consent: When you fill out a contact or career form.
  • Legitimate Interest: To protect our services and brand.
  • Legal Obligation: Where required by law enforcement or regulation.

6. Information Sharing

We do not sell your information. We may share your data with service providers for secure data handling, or with legal authorities when necessary. All sharing complies with confidentiality and legal standards.

7. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to fulfill its original purpose or comply with legal obligations. Inactive user data is periodically reviewed and deleted where appropriate.

8. Data Security

We implement physical, administrative, and technical safeguards — including SSL encryption and secure data centers — to protect your information. However, no method of digital transmission is 100% secure.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your region, you have the right to:

  • Access and review your data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion or limit use of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@singlerpartners.com

10. Third-Party Services

We may use services like Google Analytics or secure form processors. These providers have their own privacy policies which govern their data use and protection standards.

11. Children’s Privacy

Singler Partners does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has submitted data to us, please notify us and we will delete it promptly.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may revise this policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted here with a new effective date. Major updates will be communicated via our website or email notices.
