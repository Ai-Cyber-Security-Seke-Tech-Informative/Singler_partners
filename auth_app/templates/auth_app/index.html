Risk Intelligence

Our expert team gathers and analyzes critical intelligence to anticipate and mitigate threats before they impact your operations, ensuring proactive protection of assets and reputation across Africa’s complex markets. With a particular focus on both East and West Africa, we identify region-specific risks—ranging from internal fraud and counterfeiting to geopolitical instability and regulatory shifts—and deliver tailored strategies that empower decision-makers. By integrating local insights with global standards, we help clients maintain operational continuity, safeguard supply chains, and respond swiftly to emerging threats in high-risk and opaque environments.

Covert Surveillance

We deploy discreet, technologically advanced surveillance techniques to monitor suspect activities, uncover illicit trade, and protect your brand with precision, all while maintaining utmost confidentiality and legal compliance. Operating across East and West Africa, our covert operations are tailored to the unique challenges of each market, whether it's tracking counterfeit goods, documenting internal misconduct, or exposing unauthorized distribution networks. Our intelligence-driven approach ensures timely, actionable insights without disrupting your operations or alerting potential perpetrators.

Digital Forensics

Our skilled forensic specialists recover and examine digital evidence from complex cyber incidents and internal investigations, delivering robust, court-ready findings that strengthen your legal position and deter future breaches. Leveraging cutting-edge tools and region-specific expertise across East and West Africa, we trace data manipulation, unauthorized access, and insider threats with precision. Our discreet methodologies preserve chain of custody and ensure admissibility, helping clients resolve disputes, meet compliance standards, and mitigate reputational or financial fallout effectively.

Contact Us

Supply Chain Security

We deliver comprehensive risk assessments and intelligence solutions tailored to securing your supply chains from counterfeiting, theft, and operational disruption, ensuring uninterrupted business continuity in high-risk environments. By leveraging on-ground assets and regional insights across East and West Africa, we identify vulnerabilities, map illicit networks, and implement proactive safeguards. Our approach integrates strategic foresight, investigative precision, and regulatory alignment—empowering businesses to operate confidently and compliantly, even in opaque or volatile market conditions where threats are often hidden.
